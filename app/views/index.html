  • Iterate additional documents task. This broadly looks at improving general guidance, linking to resources and updating what docs are needed. E.g. conversions only need the deed of variation not the deed of novation and variation

  • Iterate process conversion grant task. This broadly looks at improving general guidance.

  • Iterate stakeholder kickoff tasks. This broadly looks at improving general guidance, removing acronyms and sometimes describing at a high level what the check should be.

  • Update key information so that all information should be populated when a project is picked up. This is information to identify the school and give a indication of when the project should be completed.
