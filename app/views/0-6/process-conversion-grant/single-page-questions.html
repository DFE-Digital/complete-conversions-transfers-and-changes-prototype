St. Wilfred's Primary School

Process conversion grant

General guidance

Check the grant payment form is ready, the school or trust has a vendor account and send the correct information to the Grant payment's team.

Start this as soon as possible (because schools incur costs?). Schools and trust can start incurring solicitor and local authority fees early in the process.

You can check if the school or trust has a vendor account (opens in new tab) on Sharepoint.
Guidance for vendor accounts

The school or trust receiving the conversion grant must have a vendor account.

Schools and trusts can set up a vendor account (opens in new tab) when they provide information about their banking and payments to DfE.

Share the link with them so they can register if needed.

Guidance for checking grant eligibility
You need to confirm the amount of money the school can receive.

This information must be included in your email to the Grants payment team later.

Make sure there's no missing information.
Guidance for checking grant eligibility

The school or trust must send you the Grant payment form.

Once they have, check the Grant payment form is complete. Make sure there's no missing information

Save the completed form in the School's SharePoint folder.

Guidance on what information to send

You need to send the following documents and information to the Grant payments team:

  • Application to convert
  • Academy order
  • Grant claim form
