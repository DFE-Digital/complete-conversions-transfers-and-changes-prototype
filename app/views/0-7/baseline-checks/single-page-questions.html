St. Wilfred's Primary School

Baseline check

You must confirm the conversion date within 3 working days of receiving the Baseline spreadsheet.
How to check the Baseline

The Academies operations team will usually email the Baseline spreadsheet to you in the first week of each month.

The Baseline spreadsheet contains a list of all the schools expected to open as academies the following month.

Look in the Baseline spreadsheet for the region the school is in. The school should appear on that list.

The Academies operations team may refer to the Baseline as the Openers list.

What to do if your school is not on the Baseline spreadsheet

If the school does not appear in the right region's Baseline, you should check the Baseline for all the other regions to see if the school has been mistakenly added to the wrong region's Baseline.

If the school is in the wrong region, remove it from that Baseline and place the information in the correct region.

If it doesn't appear at all, enter the school's information in the correct Baseline spreadsheet.

Email the Academies operations team to tell them if you have had to move or add the school to the correct Baseline.

How to confirm the school's conversion date

There is a section of the Baseline that asks if the project should stay on the Baseline.

Enter “Yes” to confirm it will convert on the target date. Enter “No” if the conversion is delayed.

If the school will not convert as planned, it will be moved to the next month's Baseline unless you specify an alternative month.
