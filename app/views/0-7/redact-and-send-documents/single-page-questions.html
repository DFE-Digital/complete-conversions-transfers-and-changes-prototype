St. Wilfred's Primary School

Redact and send documents

Documents relating to the conversion will be added to GOV.UK. These must be redacted to remove sensitive information. Once redacted, email them to the funding team at FundingAgreements.CONVERTERS@education.gov.uk.
Help redacting the documents

You need to create a redacted version of each document after they've been signed by the Secretary of State. Once redacted, save the documents in the school's SharePoint folder.

You must remove or hide:

  • names
  • signatures
  • home addresses
  • funding information
  • bank account details
  • information on the Secretary of State's seal

Required documents for each conversion:

  • Land questionnaire
  • Land registry title plans
  • Supplemental funding agreement

Depending on the project, you may also need:

  • Articles of association
  • Church supplemental agreement
  • Deed of variation
  • Direction to transfer
  • Master funding agreement
  • Trust modification order
