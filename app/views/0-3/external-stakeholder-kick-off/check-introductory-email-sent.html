Have you sent the kick-off email?
Kick-off or introduction emails should be sent to all external stakeholders. They should include documents that they will need to complete as well as any dates for milestones.
Recommended content for kick-off emails

Emails should include:

  • attached documents that external stakeholders will need to complete and return
  • a list of which stakeholder should complete each document, with an appropriate due date
  • a project end date and a list of key dates or milestones
  • explain ways of working such as monthly progress calls
  • link to forms or website that need completing such as the vendor account form
  • some options for when the kick-off call could happen
