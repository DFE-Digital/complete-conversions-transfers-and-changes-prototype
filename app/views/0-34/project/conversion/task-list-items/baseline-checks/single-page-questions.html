Check the Baseline

You must confirm the conversion date within 3 working days of receiving the Baseline email. Indicate on the Baseline that the school is on track to open.
How to check the Baseline

The Academies Operational Practice Unit will usually email the Baseline spreadsheet to you in the first week of each month.

Your projects should be on the Regional Casework Services Baseline spreadsheet. This contains a list of all the schools expected to open as academies the following month, across all regions.

Check the Regional Casework Services Baseline spreadsheet to confirm:

  • your school is on it
  • the information about the school is correct

Enter yes if the project is on track to convert, or no if it will not open on the target date.

What to do if your school is not on the Baseline

There is one Baseline spreadsheet for each region. These are only used by regional delivery officers.

Sometimes Regional Casework Services projects will show in the regional Baseline spreadsheets by accident.

If this happens, remove your school from the regional Baseline spreadsheet and add it to the Regional Casework Services Baseline spreadsheet.

If it doesn't appear at all, enter the school's information in the correct Baseline spreadsheet.

Email the Academy Operational Practice Unit at academiesdelivery.operations@education.gov.uk to tell them if you have had to move or add the school to the correct Baseline.
